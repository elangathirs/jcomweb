Training



All new members must attend the Member orientation Program in their Zone within the first 60 days of joining. Only after attending the training may the new member is added to the 'speaker rotation' for that Table. Any new members not attending the training within 60 days of induction into the Table will be subject to having their classification opened by the Executive Board.
