Members

  • Individuals who are either Active, Associate or SMA Member of JCI, can be admitted in JCOM by Annual JCOM Subscription.
  • A Table can admit only ONE person from same classification / Category of Trade, Industry or Profession
  • JCI India will have the final authority relating to classification.
  • An Individual member can join only one Live Table, only one virtual Table and unlimited number of Association Tables of Payment of corresponding subscription(s).
